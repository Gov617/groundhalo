FAQ

Quick answers for directors and admins.

Do we have to canvass inside GroundHalo?

No. GroundHalo is a visibility layer. Your team can work in VAN, NationBuilder, or other tools. GroundHalo focuses on shift + location freshness + exceptions.

How often does location update?

Typically every 5–10 minutes (configurable). The system flags stale pings and low-accuracy conditions so directors know what’s reliable.

Is tracking always on?

No. Tracking is shift-gated with clear “Tracking Active” status. The goal is trust and transparency.

Can we use turf boundaries?

Optional. If you already have turfs, GroundHalo can show on/off turf monitoring. Many teams start without it and still get huge value.
