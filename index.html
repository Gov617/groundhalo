How it works

Connect your tools

Integrate with VAN and NationBuilder so your field plan and data stay where they belong.

Run shifts in real time

Canvassers use a native mobile app for clock-in/out, turf, and quick communication.

See everything live

Directors get a single command view: who’s active, where they are, and what’s moving.

What you get in v1

Dashboard (web)

  • Live map with on-turf / off-turf status
  • Shift activity + “no ping” alerts
  • Simple KPIs by team, turf, and time
  • Exportable reports for leadership

Canvasser app (native)

  • Clock in/out and shift reminders
  • Turf assignment and navigation
  • Quick message to HQ
  • Battery-friendly location pings
